ABOUT

Labyrinthian is a brutal action platformer roguelike where reality is as fractured as the souls trapped within it. This Pygame-powered odyssey thrusts you into a world that defies logic—where subterranean dungeons can bleed into sprawling overground mushroom forests. As a marked survivor driven by an inescapable desire, you must carve your way through the shifting depths to uncover the truth of the Labyrinth.

Kill. Crumble. Conquer. Repeat. There is no turning back. The only way out is deeper.

THE STORY

THE LABYRINTH

The Labyrinth is not a destination, but a fracture in the skin of the world. When the great maws opened across the globe, they did not lead to caves or pits, but to an impossible convergence of realities where logic went to die. Within its shifting walls, the laws of existence are mere suggestions; a traveler might cross a threshold of ancient stone only to emerge into a sprawling expanse of impossible flora, or a landscape where the very elements are inverted and hostile. It is a non-Euclidean nightmare that is constantly rewriting its own geometry, ensuring that no map is ever accurate and no path remains the same once it has been walked.

THE PITBORN

Deep within the shifting strata dwell the Pitborn—entities that feel less like living things and more like errors in the fabric of the Labyrinth. They are the silent sentinels of the descent, existing in defiance of biological reason and the laws of the surface. There are those who whisper of a darker connection between the monsters and the lost; they note the way a Pitborn's movements can feel like a distorted memory, or how their presence seems to resonate with a familiar, hollow grief.

THE LABYRINTHIANS

To the world above, they are legends; to the Labyrinth, they are guests who never truly leave. Whether seeking treasure, glory, adventure, or a desperate escape from the surface, all who enter are eventually bound by the "Labyrinth's Call"—a maddening drive to reach the bottom. Here, death is a broken promise; revival is certain, but each return erodes the soul until the original motive is forgotten. While history celebrates a rare few who "conquered" the depths, a hollow silence remains in their eyes. For those who have heard the Call, the surface is merely a waiting room for an inevitable return to the abyss.

LABYRINTHIANS

The Labyrinth beckons saint and sinner alike. In the dark, you will encounter fellow Labyrinthians clinging to fraying goals and desperate desires. Whether they offer a steady hand or are merely hollow echoes of the surface, a shared path may be the only thing keeping the madness at bay.

The Desperate Sovereign

"Let the Labyrinth take my mind, let it twist my flesh, let it steal my very name—if the price of my soul is a future for my people, I will pay it a thousand times over"

The Sovereign entered the fracture not for glory, but for a cure. With his kingdom perishing and his treasury empty, he seeks the Primal Spark—an artifact of life hidden in a place of death. Now, he must choose: save his people from the plague, or save his own soul from the Labyrinth's growing rot.

The Crowned Hollow

"Still clutching hope like a shield? I've seen the end of your path and the start of your return; both are graveyards. Put down your steel. The Labyrinth doesn't let go once it's seen your face."

A living myth, The Crowned Hollow is one of the few to have ever escaped the Labyrinth—only to find the surface world cold and hollow. Now, a relentless voice from the depths calls his name, a pull he can no longer resist. He has returned to the fracture not as a hero, but as a prisoner of that beckoning cry.

The Eutrophized

"I have watched countless Labyrinthians fall, only to stand and call it 'another chance.' How naive. Tell me, Wanderer... do you truly believe death here costs nothing?"

The Eutrophized outgrew the surface world and regarded it as a fragile dream centuries ago, choosing instead to breathe with the Labyrinth's stagnant flow. She watches with quiet pity as Labyrinthians revive, knowing each death is futile—for to enter these depths is to seal one's fate, trading the soul for a permanent seat in the deadwater.

GAMEPLAY

THE KINETIC CYCLE

Traversing the Labyrinth is a deadly dance where you must master fluid dashes, double jumps, and wall-kicks to turn the environment into your momentum. However, death is not an ending, but a brutal reset; when your life is spilled upon the stone, you are cast back to the surface to begin your descent anew. Each run forces you to start from the top, testing whether your resolve is strong enough to survive the climb back down into the suffocating dark.

THE SHIFTING DOMAIN

The Labyrinth is a living machine that reconfigures its terrain with every run. No two paths are ever the same, and each unique biome is guarded by a monstrous boss that embodies the cruel logic of its domain. To reach the heart of the fracture, you must adapt to ever-changing hazards and defeat the apex sentinels that stand as the ultimate gatekeepers of the labyrinth.

GIFTS OF THE LINGERING

You are not entirely alone in the silence. Scattered throughout the shifting halls are Labyrinthians who grant you powerful buffs and ancient boons. These lingering souls offer the only edge you have against the rising tide, providing the strength needed to navigate the impossible architecture. Listen to their whispers and accept their mercy, for their gifts are the only fragments of humanity left in a world of cold, unmoving stone.

THE CREATOR

Ma. Tiffany Anne B. Bato is the sole developer of Labyrinthian. Currently a first-year IT student at the University of Dagupan, she built the game entirely in Python using the Pygame and PyTMX modules. To bring the world of the Labyrinthian to life, she utilized Aseprite for pixel art and Tiled for intricate level design.
